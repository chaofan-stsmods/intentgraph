Show monster intents as a state graph.

[h1]Intent[/h1]
An icon that showing next action of the monster.

[h1]Intent set[/h1]
A group of icons wrapped in a blue rectangle that showing next possible actions of the monster.
In the top left corner of the icon is the possibility of that intent.
In the top right corner of the icon is the maximum times that the intent can be used in a row.

[h1]Transfer[/h1]
* Start from the top left intent or intent set.
* Next turn, follow yellow arrow to next intent or intent set.
* If condition on a red arrow is met, follow it to next intent or intent set instantly.

将怪物意图显示为状态机。

[h1]意图[/h1]
显示怪物下次的行动。

[h1]意图组[/h1]
用蓝色方框框起来的一组意图，是怪物下次所有可能的行动。
左上角是概率。
右上角是这个意图最多能连续使用的次数。

[h1]意图变化[/h1]
* 总是从左上角的意图或意图组开始。
* 下回合，沿黄色箭头去下个意图或意图组。
* 如果红色箭头的条件满足，立刻去它指向的下个意图。
